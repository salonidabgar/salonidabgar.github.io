About Me

I'm Saloni Dabgar, a Software Developer at Jaguar Land Rover with expertise in automotive software systems and embedded development. Currently working on cutting-edge vehicle communication systems and Software Over-The-Air (SOTA) updates, I bring a strong foundation in C++, Python, and blockchain technology.

My experience spans from developing E2E Headlamp systems and UDP communication interfaces to implementing secure HTTPS servers with OpenSSL. I've also contributed to blockchain projects at the University of Zurich, creating smart contracts and developing currency exchange platforms.

With a unique blend of automotive software expertise and full-stack development skills, I specialize in creating efficient, secure, and scalable solutions. My background in machine learning and deep learning allows me to bring innovative approaches to complex technical challenges.

I'm passionate about writing clean, maintainable code and have a proven track record of delivering high-performance solutions that improve system efficiency and user experience. My technical toolkit includes C++, Python, React, Docker, and various DevOps tools.

I'm always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of technology, whether in automotive software, blockchain, or full-stack development.
